* Common Misconception - Accessibility means boring design and clunky interfaces.

* Color Contrast 
    WCAG Recommendations - Backgroung to text color contrast ratio
        1. Small text (23px or less): 4.5
        2. Small bold text (17px or less): 4.5
        3. Large text (24px or more): 3
    
    * webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker
    * color.adobe.com/create/color-accessibility

* Color Blindness
    1. Up to 8% of the male population are red-green color blind
    2. There are several other types of color blindness, some of which primarily affect the female population
